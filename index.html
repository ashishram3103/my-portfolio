About

I'm a UI/UX Engineer and Frontend Developer based in Bengaluru — I build complete web products from design concept to live deployment, solo.

My work spans the full stack: UI/UX engineering, frontend motion, backend integration, brand systems, and AI-assisted workflows. I translate ideas directly into functional, performant experiences — no handoff loss, no agency overhead.
